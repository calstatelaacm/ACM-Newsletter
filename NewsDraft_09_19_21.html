JPL 2022 Summer Internship - Software Engineering

JPL is seeking highly motivated college and university students with intellectual curiosity to join our team and aid in our quest to ensure mission success. In this role you would be a vital team member in a JPL software or computer engineering groups. You will partner with lead engineers to craft, code, and develop secure software applications and interface solutions that power JPL’s spacecraft, science instruments, mission control systems, science analysis pipelines and IT services.

Requirements:

  • Minimum Cumulative 3.0 GPA (om 4.0 scale)
  • Must be available to work full-time (40 hours/week) for 8-12 weeks during the Summer
  • Relevant internship experience
  • Good verbal and written communications skills with ability to work in a team environment

STEM Advantage Scholars Program

STEM Advantage is a nonprofit organization that mentors, prepares and inspires women and underserved communities to pursue STEM careers. STEM Advantage “levels the playing field” by empowering talented students, who are mostly low-income, first-generation college students, attending California State University, the largest and most ethnically diverse 4-year public university system in the nation, with access to networks, employment and economic mobility.

Benefits:

  • Paid internships to help students gain real-world experience
  • One-to-one mentors to provide a role model, support, guidance and coaching
  • Scholarships to attend classes full-time and graduate with less student debt
  • Professional development and career panels to prepare for their job search
