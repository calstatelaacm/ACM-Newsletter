Blizzard Cyber Security Intern

One of Blizzard Entertainment’s core values is “Play Nice, Play Fair” – we believe that epic gaming experiences begin with a level playing field Because of that, we passionately do everything we can to combat cheating in our games. We want YOU to join this fight! During the internship, you will get to contribute directly to the game security development efforts here at Blizzard! You will get real-world experience and advancing our technology against cheaters trying to get an advantage across our games.

Requirements:

  • Currently enrolled in a college or university and graduating December 2022 or later
  • Available for a 12-week internship starting on late May or late June
  • Candidates must apply with a resume and please include your participation in any cybersecurity events or conferences (CTFs, Defcon, etc.)

Riot Games Systems Engineering Intern (Santa Monica)

Systems Engineers at Riot play a significant role in supporting our global player base by using their expertise to build efficient infrastructure solutions. We seek to eliminate the need for human interaction with infrastructure by automating a range of functions such as orchestration, deployments, or configuration management. Systems Engineer at Riot recognize how and when to use automation frameworks like Python, Terraform, or Ansible, we understand the basic concepts of software programming, and readily craft tools to make it easier for Rioters to ship code.

Requirements:

  • Available to work full-time hours for 10-12 week at our Santa Monica Headquarters (subject to change based on COVID-19 restrictions)
  • Knowledge of data structures and software engineering paradigms
  • Experience working with Linux operating systems
  • Familiarity with cloud computing concepts
  • Understanding of distributed systems and concurrent programming

STEM Advantage Scholars Program

Applications have been extended till October 10th (Midnight). STEM Advantage is a nonprofit organization that mentors, prepares and inspires women and underserved communities to pursue STEM careers. STEM Advantage “levels the playing field” by empowering talented students, who are mostly low-income, first-generation college students, attending California State University, the largest and most ethnically diverse 4-year public university system in the nation, with access to networks, employment and economic mobility.

Benefits:

  • Paid internships to help students gain real-world experience
  • One-to-one mentors to provide a role model, support, guidance and coaching
  • Scholarships to attend classes full-time and graduate with less student debt
  • Professional development and career panels to prepare for their job search
