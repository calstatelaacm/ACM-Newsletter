    Beginner
    Website Portfolio

    Interested in game development with a hint of AI/ML? Then the beginners project is right for you! Get a chance to rebuild the original flappy bird game, but with a twist- while you can manually play the game, you'll also be able to work with machine learning concepts to build an AI that could play the game perfectly. Using Python and important concepts like object oriented programming, you'll be able to learn about designing projects and how to create a user interface with the Pygame library.


    Languages/Libraries Used
    • Python
    • PyGame

    MOBILE ENGINEER SUMMER INTERN FOR HOUSEPARTY (EPIC GAMES)

    Job Description

    Houseparty is a face-to-face video chat social network that brings empathy to online communication. As a Mobile Engineer Summer Intern, you will collaborate with other mobile developers, producers, QA, and data analysts to define and solve challenges across the iOS platform as well as build product feautres.

    Epic Games is looking for a Mobile Engineer Intern with perferable knowledge of the iOS platform, from low-level systems up to client UI.

    Requirements
    • Strong skills in Swift and a scripting language (Python, Bash, etc)
    • Projects with Games/Social preferred
    • Experience interacting with REST services (we use Scala) and responsive apps
    • Experience working on projects with teammates

    Application Deadline

    February 26, 2021

    Our Mission
    • Insight and knowledge regarding the CS programs and general advice for college students to guarantee academic progress.
    • A means to communicate experiences, concerns, and challenges in the field and in life with like minded individuals and ACM leaders to promote self-improvement.
    • Coding workshops that teach new technologies and common software engineering practices not taught in courses offered in the CS program. Professional development workshops tailored to help members prepare for the workforce.
    • A 1-1 Mentorship Program where one of our mentors will offer their knowledge and insight in the field to participating mentees.
    • Social activities, events, and competitions that promote involvement outside of campus.
    • Ultimately serve students at Cal State LA and those interested for the betterment of our Los Angeles community.
